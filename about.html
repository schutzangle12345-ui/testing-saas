Our Mission

Reliable stock data for the modern builder.

We provide stable, high-quality financial data to retail investors, developers, and educators without the enterprise price tag.

The Story

Born out of frustration.

Our journey began when our founders—a group of fintech developers and active retail investors—grew tired of the status quo. We found that most free data sources were brittle; APIs would change or break without notice, disrupting dashboards and applications overnight.

Furthermore, we realized that pulling long-term historical data was intentionally made tedious by major providers to force users into expensive subscriptions. We knew there was a better way to serve the community.

Testing-saas was built to solve three core problems: unreliable API endpoints, paywalled historical time series, and the manual formatting required to make data look presentable.
