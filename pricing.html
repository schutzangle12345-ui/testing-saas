Frequently Asked Questions

What data is actually free?

Our Basic plan provides real-time quotes for major US exchanges and up to one year of historical EOD data. It's designed for retail investors and students who need reliable data without a paywall.

How stable are the API endpoints?

We know that breaking APIs disrupt dashboards. We guarantee endpoint stability for at least 24 months before deprecation, ensuring your integrations stay functional without constant rewriting.

Can I use the charts for my blog or course?

Yes! Content creators can use our Explorer and Professional plans to generate clean visuals. Professional users can even remove attribution for a seamless brand experience.

Is there a difference in data quality between plans?

No. We use the same institutional-grade data feeds for all tiers. The difference lies in the frequency of updates, breadth of history, and export capabilities.
