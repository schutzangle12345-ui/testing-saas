Reliability & Common Questions

Quick answers to common developer and investor queries.

How do you handle API outages?

We maintain multi-region redundancy. If one data provider feed is interrupted, our system automatically switches to a backup node to ensure continuous uptime for your dashboards.

Is the $0 plan really free forever?

Yes. Our Basic plan is designed for first-time users and retail investors who need trusted data for personal use without a recurring cost.

How deep is your historical data?

We offer up to 20 years of daily EOD (End of Day) data for major US exchanges on our Explorer and Professional plans, with basic history available on the free tier.

Can I use your charts in my blog?

Absolutely. We encourage content creators to use our visuals. We just ask for a simple attribution link back to our platform.
